## ETC8M CAN Data Tx Rate

0: 200 Hz (default)

1: 50 Hz

2: 100 Hz

3: 500 Hz

Controls the rate the EGT Channels are transmitted from the ETC device.

Applied to ALL connected ETC devices

## ETC8M Fault Value

Controls the EGT value when the Input is in Fault or Open Circuit

Applied to ALL connected ETC devices
