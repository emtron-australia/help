**Ign Decel Sens Table**


Control the amount of Ignition Decel Advance for a given Rate of Change


Ignition Decel Advance = Rate of Change x Sens.


Example:&nbsp;

dTP1 = 8.0%/sec

Sens = 1.5


Ignition Advance = 8.0 %/sec x 1.5 = 12.0 of ignition advance
