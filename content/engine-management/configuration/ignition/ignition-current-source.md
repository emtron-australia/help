## Ignition Current Source

![Image](</img/Ignition4.jpg>)     

Controls the available current drive for all Ignition Channels.  Provides either 35mA at 5V or 70mA at 8.2V in high current mode.

0: Normal - 35mA

1: High Current - 70mA

Typical: 35mA

High Current Mode is commonly used for pairing outputs for the waste spark configuration,
