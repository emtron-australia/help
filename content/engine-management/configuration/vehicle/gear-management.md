 **Gear Management**

The first step to proper gear management/detection is a properly scaled and validated speed signal -> [Speed Settings](<SpeedSettings1.md>)

Gear Management in Emtron can be detected in various ways. 

Fundamentally, gear control and detection are a part of the ECU functions.
