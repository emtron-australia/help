title: "Drive By Wire"
weight: 0
---

All KV Series and SL Series ECU’s support from 1 up to 4 independently controlled Drive by Wire (DBW) Systems. 

The DBW availability is ECU based and summarized below:  
 - Shadow 8 - 1 motor
 - SL4 and SL8  – 1 motor
 - KV8 – 2 motors
 - KV12, KV16 (Rev2) – 4 motors

Select the control system and appropriate outputs from: 

Config View -> Function Setup -> Engine Functions -> DBW Control
