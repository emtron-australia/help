## VE Blend Table

VE Blend Table can be enabled under Fuel Table Control :

    Tuning -> Fuel -> Fuel Table Control

![Image](</img/NewItem354.png>)

VE Blend can blend only two VE tables at a time.

Units are 0-100%

0% = First VE Table

100% = Second VE Table
