---
title: "Global Trim"
weight: 45
---

Applies a global ignition trim. 

This trim is a +/- off the calculated ignition timing. 

It allows for a quick trim to the overall ignition timing 

and works independent of RPM and load.

Units: Deg

Min Value = -100 Deg

Max Value = +100 Deg

Typical : 0 Deg
