## Injection Mode

![Image](</img/Config Fuel1.jpg>)

0: OFF

1: Sequential

2: Sequential/Staged Sequential

3: Sequential/Staged Group

4: Non Sequential

5: GDI Sequential

6: GDI Sequential/Staged - Prim(GDI) / Sec (Port)

See [Injector Channel Setup](<injector-channel-setup1.md>) for channel setup help.

** Note In Non Sequential Mode.  Injector PW Per Cyl channels will **NOT** Calculate
