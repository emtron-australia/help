---
title: "Global Trim"
---

Applies a global ignition trim.&nbsp;


This trim is a +/- off the calculated ignition timing.&nbsp;


It allows for a quick trim to the overall ignition timing&nbsp;

and works independent of RPM and load.


Units: Deg


Min Value = -100 Deg

Max Value = +100 Deg


Typical : 0 Deg
