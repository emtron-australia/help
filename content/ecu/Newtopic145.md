**VE Blend Table**&nbsp;


VE Blend Table can be enabled under Fuel Table Control :


&nbsp; &nbsp; Tuning -\> Fuel -\> Fuel Table Control

![Image](</lib/NewItem354.png>)


VE Blend can blend only two VE tables at a time.


Units are 0-100%

&#48;% = First VE Table

&#49;00% = Second VE Table
