**Ign Accel Sens Table**


Control the amount of Ignition Accel Retard for a given Rate of Change.


Ignition Accel Retard = Rate of Change x Sens.


Example:&nbsp;

dTP1 = 5.0%/sec

Sens = 4.5


Ignition Retard = 5.0%/sec \* 4.5 = 22.5 of ignition retard.
